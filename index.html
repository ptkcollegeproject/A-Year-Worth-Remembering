About the Project

The time capsule is to be opened on Wednesday March 13, 2030. This is the date we all remember going into lockdown during spring break. It is our wish that anyone is invited to attend the event and all six campuses are invited to participate, including all faculty and staff. Members of the TCC community have donated items they feel represented this year along with the documentary and the summit recording. We were unsure of how technology might change over the years so we made the recordings available in multiple sources. We hope to reunite as a chapter and attend the opening event.
